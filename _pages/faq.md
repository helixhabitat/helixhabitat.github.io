<li class="uk-open">
<a class="uk-accordion-title" href="#">What is a zome?</a>
<div class="uk-accordion-content">
<p>A zome is a 3D architectural structure based on geometric shapes found in nature, typically made from a series of diamond or rhombus-shaped panels arranged in spiraling symmetry. Their flowing geometry creates a strong, stable shell and a naturally beautiful interior.</p>
</div>
</li>

<li>
<a class="uk-accordion-title" href="#">How are zomes different from domes?</a>
<div class="uk-accordion-content">
<p>Zomes use trapezoids instead of triangles, giving them smoother geometry and more natural flow. They shed water and snow efficiently, offer better interior layout options, and have a signature flower-like apex. Many people find them more visually harmonious and easier to insulate.</p>
</div>
</li>

<div class="uk-accordion-content">
